New Wallet

In order to use Superlumen, you need to create a wallet.
You can also open an existing wallet.

Superlumen wallets can hold multiple Stellar accounts, and provide a wealth of features to help utilize the blockchain. This software has the tooling to establish a secure wallet file, but it is up to you to keep it safe.

Superlumen is free and open-source.

Create Password

You need to secure your wallet with a password. For the best security, use a password that includes lower and upper-case letters, numbers, and special characters, and is at least 12 characters in length.

Recovery Record

You can optionally add a recovery record to your wallet. This allows you to recover your Stellar account public and private keys if you forget your password or lose your key-file.

This creates a separate attack vector to your account information and therefore needs to be setup with care. You will need at least 5 questions and answers that meet some minimum complexity requirements.
